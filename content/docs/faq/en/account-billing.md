# Account & Billing

Frequently asked questions about account management and payments.

## Account

### How do I change my password?

Go to Settings > Account Security > Change Password.

### What if I forgot my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset via email.

### How do I delete my account?

Contact support to request account deletion. We'll process it within 30 days.

## Billing

### What payment methods are supported?

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer

### How do I get an invoice?

Go to Billing Center > Invoice Management to download electronic invoices.

### Refund Policy

Annual subscribers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
