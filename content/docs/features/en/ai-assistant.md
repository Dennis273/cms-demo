# AI Assistant

MeowMail's AI assistant helps you handle emails more efficiently.

## Features

### Smart Categorization

AI automatically analyzes email content and categorizes them into appropriate folders:

- Important emails
- Work emails
- Notifications
- Marketing emails

### Smart Reply

Based on email content, AI generates multiple reply suggestions - just one click to send.

### Email Summary

For long emails, AI generates concise summaries to help you quickly understand key points.

## Enable AI Assistant

1. Go to Settings > AI Assistant
2. Enable "Use AI Assistant"
3. Select desired features

## Privacy Protection

All AI processing happens in a secure environment. We don't store or share your email content.
