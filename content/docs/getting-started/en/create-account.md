# Create an Account

Welcome to MeowMail! This guide will help you quickly create your account and get started.

## Registration Steps

1. Visit the [signup page](/signup)
2. Enter your enterprise email address
3. Set a secure password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete account setup

## Password Requirements

To ensure account security, passwords must meet the following requirements:

- At least 8 characters
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special character

## Email Verification

After registration, we'll send a verification link to your email. Please click the link within 24 hours to complete verification.

If you don't receive the verification email, check your spam folder or click "Resend verification email".

## Next Steps

After creating your account, you can:

- [Configure your domain](/docs/getting-started/configure-domain)
- [Invite team members](/docs/getting-started/invite-team)
- Start using the email service
